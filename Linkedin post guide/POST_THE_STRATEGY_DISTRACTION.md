## 📝 The Post Text

**HEADLINE:** Stop building 5-year "Strategies" when you can't handle Monday's email.

**BODY:**
I see so many leaders obsessed with their "AI Roadmap."
While this is important, it is not urgent.

They have slide decks. They have vision statements.
They want to "Revolutionize the Industry."

But then you look at their daily operations:
• Their team is drowning in manual scheduling.
• Their "CRM" is a mess of duplicate contacts.
• They spend 2 hours a day searching for files.

You can't build a Skyscraper on a swamp.
If your core operational hygiene is messy, adding "Advanced AI" just scales the mess and takes up valuable time.

Real innovation isn't about the shiny new thing.
It's about having the discipline to fix the boring, broken things right in front of you.
