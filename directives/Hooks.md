# Hooks Library

A strong hook creates curiosity, generates emotion, or establishes authority by offering immediate value. 
It must be a scroll-stopping first line, followed by a "re-hook" in the second/third line to keep them reading after they click "...see more."

When generating posts, select the most appropriate Hook category based on the post's objective, topic, and style. Use or adapt one of the examples/templates provided in that category.

## 1. Audience Targeting (Call out your tribe)
**Purpose:** Create urgency for a specific group about to do a common task, fix a pain point, or break a bad habit.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Pre-Action Warning:** {Persona}, read this before your next {action}:
- **The Quick Fix:** {Persona}, fix {pain} in 3 steps:
- **The Habit Breaker:** {Persona}s — stop doing {bad_habit}. Try this:
- **The Directive:** {Persona}, your next move is {action}:
- **The Beginner’s Guide:** New {persona}? Start here →
- **The "Hard Way" Callout:** Dear {persona}, you're making this harder than it needs to be:
- **The Problem-Solution Hook:** Feeling overwhelmed by {specific pain point or daily challenge}?
- **The FOMO Hook:** Everyone’s talking about {latest industry trend}—here is what you need to know.

## 2. Case Studies (Proof & Results)
**Purpose:** Show direct correlations between actions and desired outcomes, highlighting wins or sharing your journey.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Method Reveal:** How I {result} with {topic}:
- **The Unconventional Win:** My client {impressive_result} in {timeframe} — no {common_requirement}.
- **The Journey Hook:** Behind the scenes: {start_point} → {end_point}:
- **The Asset Conversion:** I turned {asset} into {result}. Here's the system:
- **The Top Performer:** Ranked top 1% in {context}. Habits below ↓
- **The 10,000 Hours Hook:** {Large_number}+ hours of {activity} taught me this truth:
- **The Case Study Hook:** We tested {number} {topic} strategies. One had {percentage} more impact.
- **The Behind-the-Scenes Hook:** Ever wondered how we {achieve an impressive process}?
- **The Massive Growth Hook:** I went from {starting metric} to {massive metric} in {timeframe}.

## 3. Contrarian & Myths (Stop the scroll with tension)
**Purpose:** Challenge common industry advice, bust myths, or state an unpopular opinion to create friction and debate.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Pivot:** Most people {common_advice}. I {contrarian_approach}.
- **The Disagreement:** "You need to {popular_advice}." I disagree! Here is why:
- **The Reality Check:** Everyone says {wisdom}. But here is what actually works:
- **The Unpopular Opinion:** Unpopular opinion: {opinion}
- **The Anti-Requirement:** You don't need {sacred_cow}. You need {opposite}.
- **The Data-Backed Truth:** Myth: {myth}. Reality: {truth} (data inside).
- **The Reframe:** It's not {myth}. It's {truth}.
- **The Challenging Hook:** {Common practice} is dead. Here’s why {new approach} is the future.
- **The Myth-Busting Hook:** Everyone says you need to {common advice}. But I did {unexpected alternative}.

## 4. Frameworks & "How To" (Value-first)
**Purpose:** Promise a structured, easy-to-follow guide or a proven system to save time and effort.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Playbook:** My {n}-step playbook for {result}:
- **The Legal Theft:** Steal my process to {result}:
- **The Operating System:** Operating system for {topic} in {n} bullets:
- **The Time-Saving Framework:** The {n}-minute framework that changed everything:
- **The Obstacle Bypass:** How to {result} in {timeframe} without {obstacle}:
- **The Tiny Tweak:** One tiny {change} fixed our {metric}:
- **The Lazy Guide:** The lazy person's guide to {result}:
- **The “How-To” Hook:** Want to improve your {specific skill/asset} in just {short timeframe}?
- **The Fast-Track Hook:** It took me {long timeframe} to master {skill}. I’ll explain it in {short format}.
- **The Interview/Process Hook:** When I {perform routine task}, I always use this one method:

## 5. Data & Stats (Hard evidence)
**Purpose:** Use numbers, mass analysis, or historical data to grab attention and establish immediate credibility.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Mass Analysis:** I analyzed {large_number} {items}. Here is what I found:
- **The Historical Data:** {Timeframe} of data reveals {surprising_insight}:
- **The Undeniable Proof:** The numbers don't lie: {insight}
- **The Micro-Lift:** {n}% lift from one tweak in {topic}:
- **The Experiment Results:** {n} days, {n2} experiments — the winners:
- **The Statistics Hook:** Did you know that {surprising metric}% of {target demographic} never {expected action}?

## 6. Quick Bites (Punchy & Short)
**Purpose:** Use extreme brevity, punchy summaries, or a simple equation to stand out.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Minimalist:** Do less. Win more. Example: {case}.
- **The N-Word Summary:** {n} words: {punchy_phrase}.
- **The Scroll Stopper:** Stop scrolling if you want {result}.
- **The ROI Phrase:** {n} words that {multiplier}x my {metric}.
- **The Brutal Truth:** Brutal truth: {truth}.
- **The Simple Equation:** One {item} = {result}.
- **The Question Hook:** Ever {action related to audience} and {frustrating outcome}?
- **The Funny Hook:** {Industry-specific joke}. But seriously, {transition to insight}.
- **The Announcement Hook:** It’s finally here! After {timeframe}, I'm excited to announce {launch}...

## 7. Story & Lessons (The human element)
**Purpose:** Build deep trust through vulnerability, hard-earned lessons, and personal narratives.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Costly Mistake:** I {costly_mistake} so you don't have to. Here is what went wrong:
- **The "Then vs. Now":** {Timeframe} ago I was {negative_state}. Today I'm {positive_state}.
- **The Hard-Earned Lesson:** The lesson I learned the hardest way in {topic}:
- **The Breaking Point:** I almost quit {topic} the day this happened:
- **The Retrospective:** Things I wish I knew before {action}:
- **The Personal Story Hook:** I just wrapped up {significant milestone} that took {amount of effort}.
- **The Emotional Hook:** {Visceral emotional reaction}. I couldn’t believe {unexpected situation}...
- **The Confession Hook:** I have a confession to make about {highly debated topic}.
- **The "Friend in Need" Hook:** A {colleague/peer} was struggling to {achieve goal}. I told them…
- **The Hindsight Hook:** If I were starting {venture} over, here’s what I’d do differently.
- **The Urgent Call Hook:** My {important contact} called me frantically yesterday about {issue}.
- **The Motivating Hook:** {Desired outcome} doesn’t come from {misconception}, it comes from {true driver}.
- **The Quote Hook:** "{Provocative quote}." – {Author}

## 8. Free Templates (The lead magnet)
**Purpose:** High-value offers, starter kits, or swipe files that drive immediate inbound interest.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Direct Giveaway:** Steal my {topic} templates:
- **The Swipe File:** {n} swipe-worthy {assets} you can copy today:
- **The Starter Kit:** Quickstart kit: {topic}
- **The Full System Copy:** Copy my entire {system} ({software_tool} included):
- **The Tech Setup:** My {tool} setup that saves {timeframe} per week:
- **The Resource Hook:** Struggling with {pain point}? This {resource} helped me {result}.

## 9. Comparisons & Predictions
**Purpose:** Force a choice, declare a shift, or predict the future to establish thought leadership.
**Examples/Templates:**
- **The Versus Battle:** {Option_A} vs {Option_B}: Which wins?
- **The Paradigm Shift:** {Old_way} is dead. {New_way} is the future.
- **The Split Test:** I tested {method_a} against {method_b}. Shocking results:
- **The Crystal Ball:** My {timeframe} prediction for {topic}:
- **The Industry Warning:** {Industry} will look completely different in {timeframe}.
- **The “What If” Hook:** What if you could {achieve massive goal} without {painful requirement}?
- **The Trend Hook:** The rise of {emerging trend} is transforming {industry}.
