# CTA Category: Pre-Launch Campaigns

**File Purpose:** Use when the post builds anticipation before a product, course, or service goes live. The CTA must make getting on the list feel like an exclusive privilege, not a generic sign-up. Best at the end of tease posts, countdown content, or "coming soon" announcements.

---

1. **The VIP List Invite**
Purpose: Creates a sense of exclusivity and gets people to opt in early.
Example: Get your exclusive first look — sign up now before spots are gone!
Template: Get your exclusive first look!

2. **The Taste-and-Tease**
Purpose: Signals more value is coming and positions the list as the only way to get it.
Example: This was just a taste — get on the list for the full enchilada!
Template: This was just a taste, get on the list for the full enchilada!

3. **The Trendsetter CTA**
Purpose: Appeals to the audience's desire to be first and ahead of the crowd.
Example: Be a trendsetter — sign up now and get yours before anyone else.
Template: Be a trendsetter, sign up now and get yours before anyone else.

4. **The VIP Rope**
Purpose: Makes joining feel effortless while framing it as an exclusive access.
Example: No velvet rope required. Sign up to the VIP list now.
Template: No velvet rope required! Sign up to the VIP list now.

5. **The Social Tag**
Purpose: Drives reach through tagging while building pre-launch excitement.
Example: Tag your best friend — if they don't respond in 5 mins, they're buying you this!
Template: Tag your <best friend | boyfriend | girlfriend | sibling>. If they don't respond in 5 mins, they have to buy you this!

6. **The Early Bird Discount**
Purpose: Combines urgency and financial incentive to drive immediate sign-ups.
Example: Early bird gets the worm — and 20% off. Sign up now for early access.
Template: Early bird gets the worm, but you get 20% off! Sign up now for early access.

7. **The Countdown CTA**
Purpose: Creates real urgency by showing the clock is running out.
Example: 3 days until it drops! Not on the list? DM us your email and we'll get you on it!
Template: 3 days until it drops like it's h-o-t! 🔥 Not on the list? DM us your email and we'll get you on it!

8. **The Come-Back Hook**
Purpose: Builds a reason to return tomorrow — keeps the audience engaged across days.
Example: Come back tomorrow for a big announcement!
Template: Come back tomorrow for an exciting announcement!

9. **The Queue Jumper**
Purpose: Rewards existing subscribers and creates FOMO for those not yet on the list.
Example: Already on the list? You're at the front of the queue — everyone else, get moving!
Template: If you're on the list already you can skip the entire way to the front of the queue, you're in first.

10. **The Last Chance CTA**
Purpose: Forces a decision with a hard deadline — either they act now or miss out entirely.
Example: Last day to sign up — course starts tomorrow. Are you in?
Template: Last day to sign up, course starts tomorrow, are you in?
