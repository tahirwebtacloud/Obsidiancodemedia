# CTA Category: Selling a Service

**File Purpose:** Use when the post is from a service-based business and the goal is to drive inquiries, discovery calls, or direct conversions. The CTA must feel like a natural next step — not a hard sell. It should remove friction and make reaching out feel easy and obvious. Best at the end of authority posts, educational content, or storytelling posts that demonstrate expertise.

---

1. **The Creative Invitation**
Purpose: Uses playful language to invite inquiries without feeling salesy.
Example: Show us your brief — we'd love to work on your next project!
Template: Show us your briefs! 👙

2. **The Full Menu**
Purpose: Directs the reader to explore the full range of services available.
Example: See what else we can do — tap through now to see our full services list.
Template: See what else we can do, tap through now to see our full list of services, designed to help you in every way.

3. **The Power Session Booking**
Purpose: Positions the call as high-value and outcome-driven from the start.
Example: Get started — book your power session now!
Template: Get started! Book your power session now!

4. **The Challenge Offer**
Purpose: Invites engagement in comments and turns replies into warm leads via DM.
Example: Not sure what you need? Drop your biggest challenge below and I'll DM you some thought starters.
Template: Not sure what you need? Tell me your biggest challenge below and I'll DM you with a few thought starters.

5. **The Collaboration Pitch**
Purpose: Frames the CTA as a partnership rather than a transaction.
Example: Let's start something together — email me for a quote!
Template: Let's start a new project together, email me for a quote!

6. **The Free Trial**
Purpose: Removes all financial risk by letting the reader experience the service first.
Example: Give our platform a try completely FREE — no credit card needed.
Template: Give {product/service name} a try for FREE.

7. **The Discovery Call**
Purpose: Frames the conversation as a value-first session, not a sales call.
Example: Book your discovery call today — together we'll map out your growth plan.
Template: Book your discovery call today and together we'll explode your business in 2024.

8. **The Diary Opener**
Purpose: Creates genuine availability and makes booking feel personal and immediate.
Example: I've cleared my diary on Thursday afternoon — DM me a time for a quick 15-minute call.
Template: What does this mean for you? I've cleared my diary next <Tuesday> <afternoon>, DM me a time that suits for a quick 15 minute call and I'll talk you through it.

9. **The Appointment Rescue**
Purpose: Frames the CTA as coming to the reader's aid — removes pride barrier.
Example: Help is on the way — click the link in bio to book and we'll sort this for you.
Template: Good news, help is on its way! Click link in bio for an appointment and we'll save you from ______.

10. **The Seasonal Planning CTA**
Purpose: Uses a future deadline to make planning ahead feel urgent and smart.
Example: Now is the perfect time to get ahead — book a free consultation before the rush hits.
Template: Now is the best time to plan ahead. Head to link in bio to book in a free consultation so we can get you ready in time for _________.

11. **The ROI Preview**
Purpose: Makes the value of the service concrete before the reader even clicks.
Example: A service like ours could save you 10 hours a week and give you back your evenings. See our Services page — link in bio.
Template: A service like ours could save you ______ and give you back ______. Check out our 'Services' page on our website – link in bio.

12. **The Testimonial Bridge**
Purpose: Points to social proof before asking for the action — trust first, CTA second.
Example: Clients have gone from zero to fully booked in 60 days — check out the Testimonials highlights.
Template: A _____ in just _____?! It can be done! Find out how our past clients have achieved ____ by checking out the 'Testimonials' story highlights.

13. **The Motivational Sign-Up**
Purpose: Uses a playful inspirational nudge to lower the barrier to booking.
Example: You've got this! That was your sign to finally book — download our app and get started.
Template: You can do it! There, that was your inspirational quote to get you motivated to sign up to our ____ – download our app (link in bio) to book!
