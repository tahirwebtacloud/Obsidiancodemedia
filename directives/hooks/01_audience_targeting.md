# Hook Category: Audience Targeting (Call Out Your Tribe)

**File Purpose:** Use when the post speaks directly to one specific persona with a clear, relatable problem — a task they're about to do wrong, a habit they need to break, or a pain point they're stuck on. The hook must make the reader feel this post was written exclusively for them.

---

1. **The Pre-Action Warning**
Purpose: Creates urgency for a specific group about to do a common task.
Example: Copywriters, read this before your next email blast:
Template: {Persona}, read this before your next {action}:

2. **The Quick Fix**
Purpose: Promises a fast, specific solution to a known audience pain point.
Example: Founders, fix your churn rate in 3 steps:
Template: {Persona}, fix {pain} in 3 steps:

3. **The Habit Breaker**
Purpose: Calls out a common mistake and offers an immediate pivot.
Example: Marketers — stop doing blind outreach. Try this:
Template: {Persona}s — stop doing {bad_habit}. Try this:

4. **The Directive**
Purpose: Assumes authority and tells the audience exactly what they need to do.
Example: Freelancers, your next move is raising your rates:
Template: {Persona}, your next move is {action}:

5. **The Beginner's Guide**
Purpose: Captures newcomers looking for a reliable starting point.
Example: New social media manager? Start here →
Template: New {persona}? Start here →

6. **The "Hard Way" Callout**
Purpose: Empathizes with a struggle while promising an easier path.
Example: Dear recruiters, you're making this harder than it needs to be:
Template: Dear {persona}, you're making this harder than it needs to be:

7. **The Problem-Solution Hook**
Purpose: Calling out a pain point makes readers feel understood immediately.
Example: Feeling overwhelmed by your to-do list?
Template: Feeling overwhelmed by {specific pain point or daily challenge}?

8. **The FOMO Hook**
Purpose: Pushes people to stay informed and not miss out on trends.
Example: Everyone's talking about LinkedIn's latest algorithm update — here is what you need to know.
Template: Everyone's talking about {latest industry trend}—here is what you need to know.
