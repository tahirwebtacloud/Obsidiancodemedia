# Hook Category: Frameworks & "How To" (Value-First)

**File Purpose:** Use when the post delivers a step-by-step guide, a repeatable playbook, or a proven system. The hook must make the value feel immediately accessible and practical — the reader should think "I can use this today." Vague promises fail here; specificity in the outcome or time-saving is what makes the reader click.

34. **The "How-To" Hook**
Purpose: A clear promise of actionable value makes people click "See more."
