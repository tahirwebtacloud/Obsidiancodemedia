# Hook Category: Contrarian & Myths (Stop the Scroll with Tension)

**File Purpose:** Use when the post challenges conventional wisdom, busts a popular myth, or states an unpopular opinion. The hook must create genuine friction — the reader should feel a jolt of disagreement or surprise. Avoid soft contrarianism; the tension must be bold and backed up by the content that follows.

---

18. **The Pivot**
Purpose: Highlights what everyone does wrong, setting up your right way.
Example: Most people discount their services. I raise them.
Template: Most people {common_advice}. I {contrarian_approach}.

19. **The Disagreement**
Purpose: Directly challenges common industry advice to create friction.
Example: "You need to post 5 times a day." I disagree! Here is why:
Template: "You need to {popular_advice}." I disagree! Here is why:

20. **The Reality Check**
Purpose: Separates popular fluff from actual, working strategies.
Example: Everyone says follow your passion. But here is what actually works:
Template: Everyone says {wisdom}. But here is what actually works:

21. **The Unpopular Opinion**
Purpose: Signals a hot take that encourages debate in the comments.
Example: Unpopular opinion: Cold calling is highly effective.
Template: Unpopular opinion: {opinion}

22. **The Anti-Requirement**
Purpose: Relieves pressure by removing a supposed "must-have" requirement.
Example: You don't need a fancy website. You need a strong offer.
Template: You don't need {sacred_cow}. You need {opposite}.

23. **The Data-Backed Truth**
Purpose: Uses facts to destroy a widely held false belief.
Example: Myth: Email is dead. Reality: 40% open rates (data inside).
Template: Myth: {myth}. Reality: {truth} (data inside).

24. **The Reframe**
Purpose: Corrects the audience's perspective on a core concept.
Example: It's not a traffic problem. It's a conversion problem.
Template: It's not {myth}. It's {truth}.

25. **The Challenging Hook**
Purpose: A bold, provocative claim creates friction that stops the scroll.
Example: Traditional networking is dead. Here's why online is the future.
Template: {Common practice} is dead. Here's why {new approach} is the future.

26. **The Myth-Busting Hook**
Purpose: Challenges what people think they know, grabbing their attention.
Example: Everyone says you need to post daily. I grew with 2 posts a week.
Template: Everyone says you need to {common advice}. But I did {unexpected alternative}.
