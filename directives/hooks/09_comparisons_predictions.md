# Hook Category: Comparisons & Predictions

**File Purpose:** Use when the post draws a clear contrast between two paths, tools, or approaches — or when making a bold claim about where an industry is heading. The hook must feel decisive and authoritative, not wishy-washy. Use for thought leadership posts, trend analyses, or head-to-head comparisons backed by data or experience.

---

71. **The Versus Battle**
Purpose: Forces the audience to pick a side, driving heavy comment engagement.
Example: SEO vs Paid Ads: Which wins in 2026?
Template: {Option_A} vs {Option_B}: Which wins?

72. **The Paradigm Shift**
Purpose: Declares a permanent change in the industry landscape.
Example: Cold calling is dead. Social selling is the future.
Template: {Old_way} is dead. {New_way} is the future.

73. **The Split Test**
Purpose: Settles an industry debate using actual, tested data.
Example: I tested short form against long form. Shocking results:

74. **The Crystal Ball**
Purpose: Establishes thought leadership by calling the future of a trend.
Example: My 5-year prediction for artificial intelligence:
Template: My {timeframe} prediction for {topic}:

75. **The Industry Warning**
Purpose: Creates urgency for professionals to adapt or get left behind.
Example: Real estate will look completely different in 3 years.
Template: {Industry} will look completely different in {timeframe}.

76. **The "What If" Hook**
Purpose: Triggers imagination about new possibilities.
Example: What if you could double engagement without posting 3 times a week?
Template: What if you could {achieve massive goal} without {painful requirement}?

77. **The Trend Hook**
Purpose: Attracts attention because people want to stay relevant.
Example: The rise of AI tools is transforming content creation.
Template: The rise of {emerging trend} is transforming {industry}.
