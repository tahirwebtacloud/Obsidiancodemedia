# Hook Category: Quick Bites (Punchy & Short)

**File Purpose:** Use for short posts, announcements, or content where the entire value lands in under 10 words. The hook IS the post — no warm-up, no filler. Maximum punch, minimum words. If the post body is long, do not use this category.

---

43. **The Minimalist**
Purpose: Uses extreme brevity to stand out in a sea of long paragraphs.
Example: Do less. Win more. Example: Apple.
Template: Do less. Win more. Example: {case}.

44. **The N-Word Summary**
Purpose: Creates intense curiosity by limiting the core message to a few words.
Example: 3 words: Consistency beats talent.
Template: {n} words: {punchy_phrase}.

45. **The Scroll Stopper**
Purpose: A direct command that interrupts the user's mindless scrolling habit.
Example: Stop scrolling if you want to retire by 40.
Template: Stop scrolling if you want {result}.

46. **The ROI Phrase**
Purpose: Shows massive leverage from a very small input.
Example: 5 words that 3x my closing rate.
Template: {n} words that {multiplier}x my {metric}.

47. **The Brutal Truth**
Purpose: Delivers a harsh but necessary reality check.
Example: Brutal truth: Your product isn't as good as you think.
Template: Brutal truth: {truth}.

48. **The Simple Equation**
Purpose: Distills a complex outcome into a one-to-one relationship.
Example: One great hire = scaling to 7 figures.
Template: One {item} = {result}.

49. **The Question Hook**
Purpose: Instantly sparks curiosity and makes readers reflect.
Example: Ever send a LinkedIn connection request and never heard back?
Template: Ever {action related to audience} and {frustrating outcome}?

50. **The Funny Hook**
Purpose: Breaks the monotony of the feed and makes readers instantly like you.
Example: Why don't skeletons fight each other? They don't have the guts. But seriously — your content strategy might.
Template: {Industry-specific joke}. But seriously, {transition to insight}.

51. **The Announcement Hook**
Purpose: Big news creates instant excitement and curiosity.
Example: It's finally here! After 3 years, we're excited to launch…
Template: It's finally here! After {timeframe}, I'm excited to announce {launch}...
