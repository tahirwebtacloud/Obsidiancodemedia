# Hooks Library — Index

A strong hook creates curiosity, generates emotion, or establishes authority.
It must be a scroll-stopping first line, followed by a "re-hook" in the second/third line.

**How to use:** Read this index, pick the ONE category that best matches the post's objective and topic, then open only that file.

| File | Category | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| `01_audience_targeting.md` | Audience Targeting | Calling out a specific persona, fixing a pain point, breaking a bad habit |
| `02_case_studies.md` | Case Studies & Results | Proof-driven posts showing wins, journeys, or client results |
| `03_contrarian_myths.md` | Contrarian & Myths | Challenging common advice, busting myths, stating unpopular opinions |
| `04_frameworks_howto.md` | Frameworks & How-To | Step-by-step guides, playbooks, proven systems, value-first content |

| `08_free_templates.md` | Free Templates & Lead Magnets | Giveaways, swipe files, starter kits, resource-sharing posts |
