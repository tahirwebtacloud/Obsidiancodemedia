# Hook Category: Free Templates & Lead Magnets

**File Purpose:** Use when the post is giving something away — a template, a checklist, a resource, a swipe file, or a full system the reader can copy immediately. The hook must make the giveaway feel irresistible and instantly useful. The reader should think "I need this right now." This category also works as a hook before directing to a DM or link in bio.

---

65. **The Direct Giveaway**
Purpose: High-value offer that drives immediate inbound interest.
Example: Steal my cold email templates:
Template: Steal my {topic} templates:

66. **The Swipe File**
Purpose: Offers ready-to-use assets for immediate application.
Example: 10 swipe-worthy hooks you can copy today:
Template: {n} swipe-worthy {assets} you can copy today:

67. **The Starter Kit**
Purpose: Packages multiple resources into one irresistible bundle.
Example: Quickstart kit: SEO for beginners
Template: Quickstart kit: {topic}

68. **The Full System Copy**
Purpose: Offers the ultimate transparency and value by giving away the whole farm.
Example: Copy my entire content calendar (Google Sheet included):
Template: Copy my entire {system} ({software_tool} included):

69. **The Tech Setup**
Purpose: Attracts audiences looking to optimise their personal workflows.
Example: My Notion setup that saves 10 hours per week:
Template: My {tool} setup that saves {timeframe} per week:

70. **The Resource Hook**
Purpose: Positions you as helpful and knowledgeable without being salesy.
Example: Struggling with time management? This ebook helped me go from overwhelmed to focused.
Template: Struggling with {pain point}? This {resource} helped me {result}.
