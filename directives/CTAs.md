# Call to Action (CTA) Library

When generating posts, select the most appropriate CTA category based on the post's objective, topic, and style. Use or adapt one of the examples provided in that category.

## 1. Pre-Launch Campaigns
**Purpose:** Use these to get people to sign up to a pre-launch campaign, creating anticipation and urgency.
**Examples:**
- Get your exclusive first look!
- This was just a taste, get on the list for the full enchilada!
- Be a trendsetter, sign up now and get yours before anyone else.
- No velvet rope required! Sign up to the VIP list now.
- Tag your <best friend | boyfriend | girlfriend | sibling>. If they don’t respond in 5 mins, they have to buy you this!
- Early bird gets the worm, but you get 20% off! Sign up now for early access.
- Get an EXCLUSIVE behind the scenes sneak peek on IGTV.
- 3 days until it drops like it’s h-o-t! 🔥 Not on the list? DM us your email and we’ll get you on it! 
- Watch our Stories to see why influencers LOVE them.
- Sign up now for an early bird discount. Cheep Cheep!
- Good things come to those who wait, but great things come to those who shop the pre-sale!
- Come back tomorrow for an exciting announcement! 
- If you’re on the list already you can skip the entire way to the front of the queue, you’re in first.
- Last day to sign up, course starts tomorrow, are you in?

## 2. Promoting a Discount
**Purpose:** Use these to get people clicking on your sales and claiming offers.
**Examples:**
- YOU get a car, and YOU get a car, and YOU get… ok, so maybe we’re not Oprah, but we can give you 30% off, DM us for yours! 
- Tag 3 friends and we’ll DM you a 30% off promo-code instantly!
- 30% off? Yes, please! Applied at shopping cart, simples, shop now! 
- The sun will come up tomorrow, but this discount won’t! Offer ends midnight!
- You don’t have a shopping problem. You’re helping the economy. Need another reason to shop? Here’s 25% off sitewide. 
- Purchase something during our promotion this week and we’ll add gift wrap and a note that says ‘Congratulations, you won!’. It’ll stay between us 😉 
- We like when you push our buttons, visit our website today and get 30% off!
- SEX! Now that we have your attention, shop/click/check out our ______ for 30% off today.
- Psst…because you’ve read this far down, you get a FURTHER 10% off! Use Promocode _____ at checkout. 
- Come in store, show us this post and pocket 30% off in store. 
- Nothing haunts us like the things we didn’t buy on sale. Get it girl.

## 3. Creating Urgency (Direct Response)
**Purpose:** Direct Response Marketing to make someone do something NOW.
**Examples:**
- Don’t miss out, selling fast, click-de-click!
- Hurry! Offer ends soon!
- Final days! Swipe up now!
- Swwossshhhhhh, that’s the sound of them running out the door – grab yours now before they’re all gone!
- Don’t miss this! Only 3 spots left! 
- Join for an entire month FREE, this week only. 
- Get in quick, we’re not re-stocking these bad boys
- Claim your 7 FREE day trial now, sign up to get started.
- Offer expires at midnight, get it before we turn into a pumpkin! 
- Upgrade and save, get $50 off when you buy annually (eep, this week only!)
- You won’t want to miss this! Jump on the email list now, dropping soon!

- Not sure what you need? Tell me your biggest challenge below and I’ll DM you with a few thought starters.

- Give [product/service name] a try for FREE 
- Build your email list
- Book your discovery call today and together we’ll explode your business in 2024
- Want more followers that convert to sales? I’ve got you, let’s do it. 
- Book your demo today, visit our website and we’ll get it scheduled and in the calendar. 
- Become an ___ master, 
- Focus on what makes you money, and I’ll work on solving your ____ needs.

- You can do it! There, that was your inspirational quote to get you motivated to sign up to our ____  – download our app (link in bio) to book!

## 5. Promoting a Lead Magnet
**Purpose:** Ask people to sign up for a value-packed lead magnet (white paper, e-guide, webinar, etc.).
**Examples:**
- Seats are limited, grab your spot now! 
- Try our first module for FREE
- Binge our FREE Masterclass like it’s being taken off Netflix
- Tell me everything, and tell me now!
- For the love of the ____, grab your copy now.
- Refresh and refocus for the next month, download now.
- Grab your FREE template and get started today.
- Want the details? DM us your email address and we’ll send it right now.
- Comment reserve my seat and we’ll DM you the link to the webinar! 
- Put your proactive pants on and get organised. Get started now by checking out our free e-book.
- Everything is figureoutable with a good ______.  We’d love to send you our 10 step guide to ____ DM us your email and we’ll send it straight to your inbox.  
- Did you know, you’re only one click away from _____. Head to link in bio and we’ll send you a free ____ (valued at $__) showing you how.  

## 6. Encouraging Post Saves
**Purpose:** Get people to save your post for later to increase reach and algorithm favorability.
**Examples:**
- In the middle of something? Save this post to read it again later.
- Save this post and if it gets to 50 we’ll make it a free screensaver! 
- Save this post and if we get to 100 we’ll run a flash giveaway 
- If you’re too busy, save this post to reply later, we’ll be here!
