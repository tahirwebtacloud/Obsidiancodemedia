## Methodology

1. **Listen** - Understand the pain, not just the request. Meet with you to understand current processes, problems, and goals.
2. **Build** - Custom automation that fits specific needs. No-code/low-code. Simplest solution first. Map workflows before building.
3. **Test** - Pilot project testing one workflow (2-4 weeks). Shows measurable results with minimal risk.
4. **Launch & Train** - Complete training in simple language, easy-to-follow guides, ongoing support. Business users can make basic changes without programming knowledge.
5. **Evolve** - Gradually expand to other areas. Weekly performance reports and monthly reviews.
